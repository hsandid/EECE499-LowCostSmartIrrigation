# Wi-Fi Standards Evolution

- - 802.11b - *1999*
    - 2.4 GHz frequency
    - Bandwidth rate of 11 Mbps
    - Range of 45 meters
  - 802.11a - *1999*
    - 5 GHz frequency
    - Bandwidth rate of 54 Mbps
    - Range of 30 meters
  - 802.11g - *2003*
    - 2.4 GHz frequency
    - Bandwidth rate of 54 Mbps
    - Range of 45 meters
  - 802.11n - *2009*
    - 2.4/5 GHz frequency
    - Bandwidth rate of 300 Mbps
    - Range of 50 meters
  - 802.11ac - *2014*
    - 5 GHz frequency
    - Bandwidth rate of 433 Mbps and up
    - Range of 50 meters
